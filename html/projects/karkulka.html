červená karkulka

Godot Engine 4 beta | 2022

Červená Karkulka (or Red Riding Hood) is a is a school project I've worked on in 2022. It's a trivia game with questions about programming.

The goal is to reach the house of Karkulka's grandmother without being devoured by The Wolf that follows you everywhere you go.

Oh her way, Karkulka can step on various fields, which affect her in different ways. This is described in detail on the GitHub page.

The trivia questions are about programming, and are written in Czech. If you can't speak Czech, the game will be very difficult. Sorry.
