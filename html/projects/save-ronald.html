save ronald

Godot Engine 3 | 2021

Save Ronald is a platformer game that I've worked on in 2020 and 2021. It's made with Godot Engine 3.

It takes inspiration from games like Commander Keen, which means that it features rather difficult levels with limited ammo and platforming challenges with various enemies.

It has 8 levels in total, 7 platforming challenges and one bossfight. The game also has a neat time trial mode, which allows you to speedrun individual levels, or the entire game, and compete with your friends for the fastest times.

In addition to this all, the game features an original soundtrack by Tamkish. It's awesome and I like it a lot.

I had many more plans for this project, but alas, life often doesn't work out the way we anticipate it to. I don't expect I'll ever come back to it and finish it, unfortunately.

The game is free to download on its Itch.io page: https://oschl.itch.io/save-ronald
You can also listen to (and buy) the soundtrack here: https://tamkish.bandcamp.com/
